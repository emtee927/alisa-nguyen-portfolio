  • A LITTLE ABOUT ME

    Hi! I’m Alisa Nguyen, a designer, illustrator, and visual storyteller.

    Growing up in Vietnam and moving to Canada as a teenager has broadened my languages, cultural understanding and appreciation of diversity. If I have to define my approach to creativity in two phrases, they are

    • Playful in Spirit

      Precise in Execution

    Both come in handy in creating pop-up books, and designing works that tell stories through movement, which is one of my career goals.

    I also love combining my motion design and illustration skills in creating engaging animated stories for children.
