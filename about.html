  • A Little About Me

    Hello! I’m Alisa Nguyen, a designer, illustrator, and visual storyteller.

    Growing up in Vietnam and moving to Canada as a teenager has broadened my languages, cultural understanding and appreciation of diversity. If I had to define my approach to creativity in two phrases, they would be “playful in spirit“ and “precise in execution”.

    Both come in handy in creating pop-up books that requires deep understanding of spatial reasoning and expertise in paper-folding technologies. One of my career goals is to become a leader in creating three-dimensional designs for pop-up books that tell stories through movement.

    I also love combining my motion design and illustration skills in creating engaging animated stories for children.
