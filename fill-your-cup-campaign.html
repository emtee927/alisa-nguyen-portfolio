Fill Your Cup

✦ ILLUSTRATION & DESIGN ✦ SOCIAL GOOD CAMPAIGN

Fill Your Cup is a campaign to raise awareness among Capilano University students about food insecurity. The print brochure in the shape of an instant noodle cup opens up to reveal information about the campaign and where students can either donate food or receive it on campus.
