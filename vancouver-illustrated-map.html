Vancouver's Flavours of Vietnam

✦ VIETNAMESE RESTAURANT MAP
✦ ILLUSTRATION & MOTION DESIGN

The project highlights the Vietnamese community in Vancouver, BC, especially the diversity of Vietnamese cuisine in Canada, targeting foodie people who value the authenticity. The project is to illustrate a map of the Vancouver neighbourhood and demonstrate 6 significant location icons that will recommend the viewers some good Vietnamese restaurants, including their considerable dishes.
